Medieval Structure

Gregorian chants follow a flowing, free-form structure that relies on syllabic and melismatic phrasing. Their melodies are modal, lacking traditional harmonic direction.

Modern Modes

The Dies Irae in modern composition is still used in themes of destruction and death, but it tends to take on more bombastic ideas and emotions. These following examples, from composers Elton Johns Lion King score, John Williams iconic Star Wars score, and one of the more notorious Dies Irae in film, Wendy Carlos and Rachel Elkinds theme to The Shining, which is quite literally a Dies Irae.
